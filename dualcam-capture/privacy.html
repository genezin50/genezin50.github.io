DualCam Capture

Privacy Policy

DualCam Capture records video and photos on device. Your media is not uploaded for recording or editing.

1. On-Device Capture

DualCam Capture records with the front and back cameras locally on your iPhone. Media stays on device unless you choose to share it.

2. Camera & Photo Access

The app requests access to the camera and photo library to capture, preview, and save your content. You can change these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

3. Analytics

DualCam Capture does not upload your media or collect personal information. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated before release.

4. Contact

For privacy questions, contact genezinsalih@gmail.com.
