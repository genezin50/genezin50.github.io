Lift Log Pro

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Lift Log Pro handles app data, reminders, advertising-related services and user-controlled exports.

1. Information You Enter

Lift Log Pro may process training information that you enter into the app, including workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights, rest time, workout notes, body fat entries, workout history and related progress records.

2. Local-First App Experience

Lift Log Pro is designed as a straightforward, local-first training tracker. The app does not require account creation in order to use its core features. Training records are primarily handled for the purpose of powering the in-app experience, analytics, history views and export tools.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The app is currently free and ad-supported. Third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob may be used to support the product. These services may process certain technical data in accordance with their own privacy practices.

4. App Tracking Transparency

Where required, the app may request permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework. You are free to allow or deny that request, and your choice can be managed through your device settings.

5. Reminders and Device Permissions

Lift Log Pro includes workout reminders and rest reminders. If notification permissions are needed for these features, the app will ask through the iOS permission system and you may change that preference at any time in device settings.

6. Exported Data

The app can export workout and body-fat related data as CSV files when you choose to do so. Any exported file is generated only as a result of your own action.

7. Data Security

Reasonable technical and administrative measures are used to support data protection. However, no storage method or electronic transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure in every circumstance.

8. Children’s Privacy

Lift Log Pro is not directed to children and is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.
