Laser Eyes: Ghost Rescue Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Laser Eyes: Ghost Rescue handles information when you play. The app uses the front camera for gameplay, and face tracking happens on-device.

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Information We Collect

We do not require account creation. We may collect basic device-level information (such as device model, OS version, and app version) to keep the game stable. If we introduce analytics in the future, we will update this policy to explain what is collected.

Camera and Face Tracking

The camera is used only for gameplay. Face tracking happens entirely on your device. We do not use camera data for identity verification, and we do not create biometric profiles. Camera footage is not uploaded to our servers and is not saved by default.

How We Use Information

We use information strictly to operate and improve the game experience — such as stabilizing gameplay, improving performance, and understanding general usage patterns if analytics are enabled in the future.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share camera footage. If analytics are added later, they will be used only with trusted providers and described here.

Data Retention

Camera data is processed live and not retained. Any future analytics data will be retained only as long as needed for product improvement and will be described in an updated policy.

Children’s Privacy

Laser Eyes: Ghost Rescue is a general-audience arcade game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to remove it.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the app and any limited information we may process. No system is perfect, but we work to keep the experience safe.

Your Rights

You may request information about data we hold (if any) or request deletion. Contact us at genezinsalih@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves. The latest version will always be posted here with an updated date.

Contact Us
