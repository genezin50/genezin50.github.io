Common Topics

Is the game free to play?

Yes. Order of Colors is free with optional purchases for themes and tube styles.

How do daily challenges work?

Daily challenges refresh every day and reward coins for completing the target puzzle.

Can I play with color-blind mode?

Yes. Color-blind mode adds alternative markers for better color separation.
