Swipe Clean Photo

Privacy Policy

Swipe Clean Photo processes your media on device. Photos and videos are not uploaded for sorting and cleanup.

1. On-Device Processing

Swipe Clean Photo reviews your photos and videos locally on your iPhone. No media is sent to external servers for analysis, sorting, or deletion.

2. Photos Access

The app requests access to your photo library so it can display media for review and perform the actions you choose. You can change or revoke this permission any time in iOS Settings.

3. Deletion Actions

Items you swipe left are queued on device. Deletions happen only when you confirm a batch delete.

4. Analytics

Swipe Clean Photo does not collect personal information or upload your media. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated before release.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact genezinsalih@gmail.com.
