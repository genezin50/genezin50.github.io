Shotzin Cam

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Shotzin Cam records, edits, exports, and stores your creator content.

1. Content You Create

Shotzin Cam processes the videos, trims, cover frames, and export settings you create inside the app. This content is used to generate portrait and landscape outputs and to show your finished sessions in the in-app gallery.

2. Permissions We Request

The app may request access to the camera and microphone to record video, and Photo Library Add access when you choose to save exports to Photos. You can review or change permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

3. Local Processing and Storage

Recording, trimming, cover frame selection, and export preparation are designed to work without an account. Your media stays on your device unless you actively save or share it using iOS system tools.

4. Advertising and Tracking

Shotzin Cam includes Google Mobile Ads in the free app experience. The app may ask for tracking permission so ads can be more relevant to you. If you deny tracking, the app still works, but advertising may be less personalized.

5. Analytics and Privacy Disclosures

Data handling related to advertising or consent flows may depend on the configuration used at release time. App Privacy details in App Store Connect should be reviewed carefully before submission so they match actual SDK behavior.

6. Sharing and Exports

When you export or share a video, the action is initiated by you. Shared files are transferred through the destination you choose, such as Photos or another app via the iOS share sheet, and those services may have their own privacy practices.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact genezinsalih@gmail.com.
