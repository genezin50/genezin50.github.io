DualShot Recoder

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DualShot Recoder handles recordings, exports, and the permissions needed to deliver a fast creator workflow.

1. Content You Create

DualShot Recoder processes the videos, trims, and cover frames you create in the app. This content is used to generate portrait and landscape exports and to populate your in-app session gallery.

2. Local-First Processing

The app is designed to work without requiring an account. Your recordings and edits are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or export them.

3. Device Permissions

DualShot Recoder may request access to the camera, microphone, photo library, and local file storage. These permissions are used only to capture, edit, save, or share your videos. You can change permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

4. Sharing and Exports

When you export or share a video, the action is initiated by you. Any shared file is sent through the destination you select (for example, the iOS share sheet or another app), and those services may have their own privacy practices.

5. Children's Privacy

DualShot Recoder is intended for general creator workflows and is not designed to collect personal information from children.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
