Common issues

If the game feels off, try these quick checks:

  • Make sure the room is well-lit so the camera can see your face clearly.
  • Keep your phone at arm’s length and centered in portrait mode.
  • Restart the app after changing permissions.

How to enable camera access

Open iOS Settings → Laser Eyes: Ghost Rescue → enable Camera.

Blink or tap controls

The game supports both blink and tap. Blink to fire hands-free, or tap the screen for precision. You can switch between the two during a run.

Tracking feels inaccurate

Re-center your face, avoid harsh backlight, and keep your eyes in frame. Wipe the front camera lens if needed.

Review how the camera is used and stored data on the Privacy Policy page.
