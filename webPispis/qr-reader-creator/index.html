Privacy Policy

QR Reader Creator is an iOS QR scanner and QR code creator. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use scanning, photo reading, QR creation, history, ads, and related features.

Camera and Photos

The app uses the device camera only to scan QR codes. The app may access selected photos only when you choose an image to scan a QR code. QR Reader Creator does not upload camera images or selected photos to our servers.

Local Data

QR scan history and created QR codes may be stored locally on your device. This helps you return to codes you scanned or created, organize items with labels and categories, and use quick save or share tools.

Advertising and Tracking

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google AdMob may collect identifiers, usage data, diagnostics, and advertising-related data according to Google’s policies. The app may request App Tracking Transparency permission on iOS to show more relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can deny tracking permission and still use the app.

Accounts and Children

QR Reader Creator does not require account registration. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
