FAQ

Does TwinFrame Cam require an account?

No. You can record, trim, export, and share without creating an account.

Which formats does the app create?

TwinFrame Cam prepares 9:16 portrait and 16:9 landscape outputs from the same take.

Where are my exports saved?

Exports are saved to Photos only when you choose to save them, and you can also share them directly from the app.

Which platforms is it built for?

It is designed for TikTok, Reels, WhatsApp Status, Kwai, and YouTube workflows.
